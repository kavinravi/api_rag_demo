Employee Handbook: Work Policies and Benefits

Working Hours:
TechVentures operates on a flexible schedule. Core collaboration hours are 10 AM to 3 PM local time, with employees free to manage their remaining hours as needed.

Remote Work Policy:
We offer a hybrid work model. Employees can work remotely up to 3 days per week. Full remote positions are available for certain roles with manager approval.

Benefits Package:
- Health Insurance: Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage
- 401(k): Company matches up to 4% of salary
- PTO: 20 days annually, plus 10 company holidays
- Parental Leave: 16 weeks paid leave for all new parents
- Learning Budget: $2,000 annual stipend for professional development
- Wellness: $100/month gym membership reimbursement

Career Development:
- Annual performance reviews with clear promotion criteria
- Internal mobility program for cross-team transfers
- Mentorship matching for junior employees
- Quarterly tech talks and learning sessions
